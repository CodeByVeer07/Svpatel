About

        Shri Shambhubhai V Patel College of Computer Science and Business Management is one of the leading names in providing quality education in the fields of computer science and management. It is affiliated to Veer Narmad South Gujarat University, approved by UGC and recognized by AICTE. The institute was established in 2008 and is a trusted name with the reputation of providing the finest professionals in the fields of BCA, MBA and MCA. It aims at providing not just a solid knowledge base but also building up the confidence required to handle different situations in future.
